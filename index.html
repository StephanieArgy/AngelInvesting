Women Entrepreneurs

  • The Diana Project - Research activities, forums and scholarship focusing on women entrepreneurs and their growth.
  • Girl Meets Money: 50+ Sources for the Female Entrepreneur
  • Golden Seeds - Early-stage investment firm focused on women-led businesses.
  • Most Powerful Women Summit - Inivitation-only summit of preeminent women in business, along with leaders in government, philanthropy, education, and the arts.

Impact Investing

  • Global Impact Investing Network - Not-for-profit organization dedicated to increasing the scale and effectiveness of impact investing.
  • Investor's Circle - Non-profit early-stage impact investing network that has propelled nearly $200 million into over 300 enterprises dedicated to improving the environment, education, health and community.
  • Global Impact Investing Rating System (GIIRS) - Rigorous, comprehensive, and comparable ratings of a company or a fund’s social and environmental impact
  • SustainVC - Makes investments in the early and expansion stages of companies that provide social and environmental benefit to the world: "Patient Capital for a Sustainable Future."
  • Hitachi Foundation - Discover, demonstrate and expand business practices that improve economic opportunities for low-wealth individuals in the U.S. and enhance long term business value.
  • Ashoka - Network of social entrepreneurs worldwide, with nearly 3,000 Ashoka Fellows in 70 countries.
  • Harvard Business Review, "Every Business Is (Or Should Be) a Social Business" (January 14, 2013).

Trends and News

  • Angel Resource Institute: The Halo Report - Angel investment activity and trends in North America.
  • Pitchbook newsletters
  • TechCrunch - Technology news and information.
  • CrunchBase - Data on companies and the people behind them, originally started to track companies that had been covered in TechCrunch.
  • Angel Investing Podcast - Podcast series hosted by Pipeline fellow and angel investor Tatyana Gray.

Tools

  • Proseeder - Cloud-based platform to manage private funding opportunities and investments.
  • Gust - Global software-as-a-service (SaaS) funding platform for the sourcing and management of early-stage investments. (Used by Investor's Circle)
  • Pitchbook - M&A, PE and VC database.
